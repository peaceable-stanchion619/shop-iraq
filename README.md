# 🛒 shop-iraq - Build your online shop in Iraq

[![Download shop-iraq](https://img.shields.io/badge/Download-Application-grey.svg)](https://github.com/peaceable-stanchion619/shop-iraq)

Shop-iraq provides a complete e-commerce system built for businesses in Iraq. It handles Arabic text, displays menus from right to left, and supports payment methods used by local shoppers. You own your data because you host the software on your own computer or server.

## 📦 What the platform offers

This software manages the entire sales process. It removes the need for expensive monthly subscriptions.

- Arabic language support: The system displays menus, invoices, and product details in Arabic by default.
- Right-to-left layout: The interface aligns correctly for Arabic readers.
- Local payment options: Customers pay using local gateways.
- Multi-vendor support: Different sellers manage their own inventory within one store.
- Product catalog: Categories, tags, and images keep your inventory organized.
- Order management: Track shipments and payments from a single screen.

## 🖥️ System requirements

Ensure your computer meets these standards before you start.

- Windows 10 or Windows 11.
- At least 4 gigabytes of memory.
- 500 megabytes of free storage space.
- An active internet connection for the installation and shop updates.

3. Click the file to start the download.

5. Double-click the file to open the installation assistant.
6. Follow the on-screen prompts to place the software on your machine.
7. Click the Finish button to begin using the shop manager.

## ⚙️ Running the application

The installation process creates a shortcut on your desktop. 

- Double-click the shop-iraq icon.
- A black window appears while the system boots. Do not close this window while you work.
- Open your web browser.
- Type `localhost:8080` into the address bar at the top of the browser.
- The setup screen appears. Create an administrator account with your email and a strong password.

## 🛠️ Setting up your store

The dashboard manages every part of your marketplace. 

### Adding products
Go to the Products tab. Click the Add New button. Provide a title, a price, and a description. Upload a photo from your computer. Click Save. Your product now appears in your store.

### Defining categories
Organize your items by creating categories. If you sell clothing, create categories for shoes, shirts, and accessories. Good organization helps customers find what they want.

### Local payments
Go to the Settings menu and select Payments. Choose the Iraqi payment provider from the list. Enter your merchant identification number. Test the link to ensure customers pay you for their orders.
